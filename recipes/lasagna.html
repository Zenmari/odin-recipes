Lasagna

This is a lasagna recipe made with lasagna-based ingredients and lasagna-based methods of making lasagna. Even though this is just a practice page for The Odin Project's course, I don't want to totally plagiarize the family recipe Craig laid out. Instead, I'll list a series of lasagna-centric information about lasagna (e.g. how lasagna tastes like lasagna). In order to create lasagna, you'll likely need:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pasta
  • Lasagna (you know; to add more lasagna on top of your lasagna)

Now, you may be asking yourself, "What am I going to do with all this? How do I turn it into some delicious homemade lasagna?" Well, to turn it into lasagna, all you have to do is:

  1. Throw it into a bowl
  2. Fill the bowl with water
  3. Go down to your local store and buy some goldfish
  4. Gently drop your goldfish into an aquarium (note: it doesn't have to be your aquarium)
  5. Be nice to your goldfish. Take care of them for a few weeks, treat them with love and care
  6. After a few weeks, you can return to your runny homunculi of tomato sauce, pasta, lasagna, and tap water, and throw it out the window
  7. Simply go to your nearest Italian restaurant and buy some lasagna

It's as easy as that!
